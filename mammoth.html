MAMMOTH P-71

Starting from the ground up, the Mammoth P-71 is one of the finest hand built vehicles that delivers an unparalleled analog automotive experience. This experience is made by highlighting the most important element of a car, the driver. The Mammoth P-71 offers a connection to the driver that not only challenges them to push the vehicle to its limits, but also push themselves. As the automotive industry at large seems to want to disconnect you from your car, and in turn the road, the Mammoth P-71 seeks to reintroduce the passion that is true driving enjoyment. The Mammoth P-71 pushes the driver’s abilities in ways only recently possible on the track. Racing inspiration and aircraft influence provide the Mammoth P-71 with a handsome, dark, mysterious elegance that is sure to intimidate even the most fearsome competitors. The Mammoth P-71 raises the bar in not only performance, but in luxury, providing state of the art amenities and materials. Each engine is hand built to ensure every vehicle’s performance is tuned to exceed most expectations. The Mammoth P-71 is not content to merely compete in the automotive world, it is here to take over.
